## Key Patterns in the Space
- Biggest accounts make complex topics radically simple and visual
- Carousels = "save this" educational content; Reels = mandatory for growth
- Personal narrative (injury-to-recovery) is the most powerful hook
- Cert/app/program upsell is dominant monetization
- Evidence-based positioning is table stakes — everyone claims it
- Niche specificity wins (Squat Uni = squats, Cressey = shoulders, KOT = knees)

**Why:** Tayla's niche = "rehab + training integration, back/hips" — distinct from competitors
**How to apply:** Track these accounts weekly for format trends, topic trends, engagement patterns. Flag anything Tayla's niche can respond to.
