## Best Performing Reel Pillars

1. **"Areas of the body X aren't training (but should)"**
2. **"A lower/upper day for X"**
3. **"Your [body part] hurts because [root cause]"**
   - Your shoulder hurts because you live in internal rotation
   - Maybe your back hurts because your hips can't rotate
   - Maybe your knee hurts because your hips can't stabilise
   - Maybe your knee hurts because your foot can't stabilise
4. **"3 muscles you should be training for X pain / X exercises to help Y"**
   - e.g., Glute exercises to help tight hips
5. **"Your hip/spine/shoulder was designed to rotate"**
6. **"A muscle you're probably not training (but should)"**
7. **"X pain isn't always a X problem"**
   - e.g., Back pain isn't always a back problem
8. **"Your X was built to do more than Y"**
9. **"Instead of this, you can do this"**
   - e.g., 90/90 hip IR > fun IR, shoulder ER > fun ER
**NO "new idea" pillar** — novel ideas come from competitor repurposes and research papers instead.

## Research Exclusions
Do NOT show papers about: elderly, older adults, falls/fall risk, wrist, elbow, carpal tunnel, lateral epicondylitis
